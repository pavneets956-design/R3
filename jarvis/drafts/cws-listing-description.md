## Full Description (Plain text for CWS)

R3 surfaces subreddit rules and posting requirements directly inside Reddit — before you hit submit.

WHAT R3 DOES:

- Displays subreddit rules from Reddit's live API in a floating panel while you browse and compose
- Updates automatically when you navigate between subreddits
- Shows karma thresholds, account age requirements, and content-specific rules
- Saves per-subreddit notes in your browser (locally — nothing transmitted or stored on servers)

THE PROBLEM R3 SOLVES:

Reddit removes posts without telling you. No notification. No explanation. Your post simply disappears. This happens to new accounts especially — silent enforcement of karma thresholds, account age filters, and automod rules means you may never know why your post didn't appear.

R3 fixes this by surfacing the rules that matter before you post. You see the requirements for a subreddit without leaving the page, without scrolling the sidebar, without tab-switching.

COMING IN R3 PRO:

R3 Pro (Phase 2, coming soon) will add:
- Risk scoring: check your account stats against the subreddit's karma and age requirements
- Removal detection: find out if your post is visible to other users or was silently removed

PRIVACY & DATA:

R3 stores all data locally on your device only. No server. No analytics. No tracking. No account required.

- Subreddit rules are fetched directly from Reddit's public API
- Your notes stay on your device
- You can clear all R3 data anytime: Chrome Settings > Extensions > R3 > Clear storage

PERMISSIONS:

- storage: to save your notes and cached rules
- reddit.com access: to display the panel and fetch rules

R3 works on New Reddit (reddit.com) on Chrome and Chromium browsers. Old Reddit is not currently supported.

Have questions or feedback? Email us at r3@pavneets.com or check out the options page inside the extension.
